Main Key Terms

1.       Introduction To Project Management

Project, project attributes, PMI, PMP, stakeholders, triple constraint, Project management knowledge areas, the role of project manager

 

2.       The Project Management and Information Technology Context

Deliverable, project life cycle

 

3.       Project Management Process Groups: A Case Study

Process, Initiating processes, planning processes, Executing processes, monitoring and controlling processes, closing processes, project management process groups

 

4.       Project Integration Management

Processes of project integration management, NPV, ROI, payback, project charter, scope statement

 

5.       Project Scope Management

Processes of project scope management, decomposition, scope, work breakdown structure (WBS), WBS dictionary, scope baseline

 

6.       Project Time Management

Processes of project time management, Gantt chart, Task Dependency Types (SS, SF, FS, FF), AOA, critical path analysis, ES, EF, LS, LF, PERT, duration, time baseline

 

7.       Project Cost Management

Processes of project cost management, earned value management(EVM), AC, BAC, CV, EV, EAC, PV, SV, CPI, SPI, cost budgeting, cost baseline, life cycle costing, cost estimating, cost management plan, direct cost, indirect cost, sunk cost, management reserves, contingency reserves

 

8.       Project Quality Management

Processes of project quality management, quality assurance, Seven Basic quality tools (cause-and-effect diagram, control chart, …Pareto chart, flow chart), conformance cost, nonconformance cost

 

9.       Project Human resource Management

Processes of Project human resource management, Maslow’s hierarchy of needs, Responsibility assignment matrixes (RACI), Conflict Handling Modes (Black and Mouton), Thamhain and Wilemon’s Influence and Power, Meyers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI)

 

10.    Project Communications Management

Processes of project Communications management, Importance of Good Communications, communications channels, Reporting Performance

 

11.    Project Risk Management

Processes of project risk management, Risk-averse, risk-neutral, risk-seeking, strategies for negative risks (Risk avoidance, Risk acceptance, Risk transference, Risk mitigation)

 

12.    Project Procurement Management

Processes of project procurement management, Types of Contracts (FP, CPIF, CPFF, CPAF, T&M)

 

13.    Project Stakeholder Management

Interpersonal skills for project manager (are leadership; team building, motivation; communication; influencing; decision making; political and cultural awareness; negotiation; trust building; conflict management; coaching（指导）; the thorough self-control and the will(坚定的意志). Ref as Appendix X3 in PMBOK v5)
